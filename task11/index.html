China’s export reach, 2024

Goods exports from China to its 15 largest destination markets, in US$ billion. Line width is proportional to export value; each line runs from China (red) to the destination (blue). These 15 markets take 62% of China’s US$3.58 trillion in goods exports.

Source: UN Comtrade, China goods exports by partner, 2024. Hong Kong includes large re-export volumes. Map tiles: CARTO / OpenStreetMap.
